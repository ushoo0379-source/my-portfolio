Lighting Study Octane & C4D 2026

Technical Breakdown

Focusing on the interplay between complex geometry and physically accurate light distribution.

This study explores the limitations of Octane's spectral rendering engine within Cinema 4D. By manipulating micro-displacements and subsurface scattering (SSS), the project aims to bridge the gap between digital precision and the imperfections of organic surfaces.
