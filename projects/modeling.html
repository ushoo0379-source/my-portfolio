Project Description: Digital Resurrection

"Bridging the Gap Between Ancient Heritage and Generative Future."

This project is a visual study in Digital Resurrection, exploring the deconstruction and restoration of historical artifacts within a modern procedural framework. The core of the study is a high-fidelity reconstruction of an Armor set, focusing on the "heavy industry" of 3D production: precise hard-surface modeling and hyper realistic PBR texturing.
