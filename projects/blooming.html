Technical Narrative

"A cellular automaton manifested in 3D space, where light follows growth."

This project uses custom Vertex Displacement Shaders to create the organic, pulsing movement of the Cyber Bloom. I implemented a complex UnrealBloomPass chain to manage the high-dynamic-range glow, ensuring the "Liquid Core" maintains its visual intensity without blowing out the highlights.
