Project Narrative

"Blooming Engine" explores the symbiosis between human kinetics and digital growth.

By utilizing MediaPipe hand-tracking and Three.js, the installation translates physical gestures into biological growth algorithms. The "flower" reacts to proximity, tension, and movement, creating a unique cybernetic bloom for every participant.

Core Tech

  • Three.js / WebGL
  • MediaPipe Vision AI
  • Procedural Geometry
  • Custom GLSL Shaders
