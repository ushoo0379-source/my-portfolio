Technical Narrative

"A cellular automaton manifested in 3D space, governed by noise and light."

This project explores the intersection of Vertex Displacement Mapping and Fragment Shaders to create a living digital entity. By utilizing Three.js and custom GLSL code, I implemented a GPU-based growth simulation that reacts to user interaction in real-time.
