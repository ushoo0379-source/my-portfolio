RECURSION

Show Reel: 2 min

Project Description

"RECURSION" explores the boundaries between digital reincarnation and organic memory. This project was a deep dive into complex environment building and cinematic storytelling.

The film follows a 10-minute non-linear narrative, challenging the audience to piece together a world where data and nature converge. For this showcase, I've highlighted the most visually demanding sequences produced during my time at SVA.

Core Competencies

  • Environment Design
  • Character Animation
  • Sound Engineering
  • Octane Rendering

Sound Design Spotlight

A 1.5-minute specialized cut focusing on the foley and atmospheric scoring. I designed the soundscape to reflect the mechanical yet ethereal nature of the world, using a mix of synthesized textures and organic field recordings.
