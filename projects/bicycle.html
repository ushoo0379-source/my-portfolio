BICYCLE TOURING

2D Animation Opening Sequence 2024

Official Selection: 3rd West Lake International Animation Festival (Proposal)

Project Narrative

"Bicycle Touring" is an opening animation proposal designed for the West Lake International Animation Festival.

The animation captures the fleeting essence of youth and exploration. By using a minimalist yet expressive 2D aesthetic, the project focuses on the rhythmic harmony between the rider and the environment. It translates the physical sensation of a journey into a visual poem of motion and color.

Core Competencies

  • Visual Storytelling
  • Character Motion Design
  • Art Direction
  • Festival Sequence Branding

Visual Language

The visual style emphasizes clean lines and a soft, evocative color palette to evoke a sense of nostalgia. The motion design prioritizes "flow" over complexity, mirroring the effortless momentum of a bicycle descending a hill.
