PIG OUT

Character Animation 2026

Primary Showcase: Expressive Character Performance

Project Narrative

"PIG OUT" is a high-energy exploration of squash and stretch principles. This project focuses on exaggerated character acting and the "appetite" of motion.

Designed as a study in comedic timing, the project utilizes complex Rubber-Hose rigging and frame-by-frame facial expressions. It highlights my ability to bring a static illustration to life with personality and distinctive "snappy" movement.

Core Competencies

  • Squash & Stretch
  • Rubber-Hose Rigging
  • Facial Acting
  • Comedic Timing
